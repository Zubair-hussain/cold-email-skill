# Tone Guidelines

## Tone Style
- Human
- Casual but professional
- Direct
- Helpful (not salesy)

## Should Feel Like:
- One person writing to another
- Not a marketing email
- Not automated

## Avoid:
- "We are a leading agency..."
- "Cutting-edge solutions..."
- "We specialize in..."

## Preferred Language
- "Quick thing I noticed..."
- "I tried using your site..."
- "Most visitors will..."

## Key Principle
👉 Sound like a real human, not a company
