# Email Structure Framework

## Step 1 — Observation
Mention something real from their site

Example:
"I tried searching homes on your site..."

---

## Step 2 — Problem
Identify ONE issue

Example:
"...there’s no way to browse listings"

---

## Step 3 — Consequence
Explain what happens because of it

Example:
"Most visitors leave and go back to Zillow"

---

## Step 4 — Impact
Optional but powerful

Example:
"That’s lost opportunities every day"

---

## Step 5 — CTA
Soft curiosity-based question

Example:
"Want me to send a quick breakdown?"

---

## Important Notes
- Never skip steps
- Never add extra fluff
- Keep flow natural
